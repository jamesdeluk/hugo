## New glasses

-2.50s and -4.00s arrived. Much better. With differentials I can sit comfortably away from the computer screen (no neck pain) and be at edge of blur to exercise my eye. I even find now my vision _improves_ over the day as I stretch my eyes. The normlised make seeing distance things more comfortable, easier to read further things (but enough blur that I sometimes have to work for it). Floaters are a big issue as they distract from my focus but nothing I can do about them. Either way, these glasses are much better.

I also bought yellow-tinted clip-on lenses to go over my normal lenses. Quite good for evenings.
